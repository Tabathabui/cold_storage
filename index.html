What is a Community Fridge?

A community fridge can be a 24/7 anonymous food resource. Community fridges are established with the hopes of accomplishing three goals:

• Reduce food waste.

• Help to eliminate food insecurity.

• To build stronger community.

Mutual-Aid Starts with You




Food is not, and should never be, a privilege. We’re a network of decentralized, independent refrigerators and pantries in the Los Angeles county that provide food and vital supplies to our communities through mutual aid = neighbors helping neighbors.
